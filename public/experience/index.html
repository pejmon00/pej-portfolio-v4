Experience

These case studies reflect work on systems that operate across products, platforms, and organizations.

The focus is not on individual features, but on system-level structure—how multiple surfaces coexist, how behavior remains predictable at scale, and how decisions hold up over time. Each case examines a different layer of that problem, from foundational abstractions to global UI surfaces and embedded intelligence.
