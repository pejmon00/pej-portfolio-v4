ARTICLES

These articles are published essays written outside the context of client work.

They come from the same place as my design thinking, but move at a different pace. I’m drawn to writing traditions that approach meaning differently—Persian philosophy, Irish poetry, and both Western and Eastern ways of shaping thought through language. Some writing leans into metaphor and patience, letting meaning unfold. Other traditions favor structure, clarity, and consequence. What holds my attention lives between those approaches—where feeling and discipline coexist, and where language is used to reveal rather than persuade. Each piece here stands on its own and is presented as originally published.
